Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Feel free to ask. We are here to assist you.

What types of packages do you handle?

We handle a wide array of packages, from small parcels to large freight, ensuring safe and timely delivery for all types of shipments.

How can I track my package?

You can easily track your package using our online tracking system. Simply enter your tracking ID on our website to get real-time updates on your shipment's status and location.

What are your delivery times?

Delivery times vary depending on the destination and service selected. We offer a range of delivery options, including express and standard services, to meet your timing needs. Specific delivery times can be estimated when you book your shipment.

What if I need to change my delivery address?

If you need to change your delivery address, please contact our customer support as soon as possible. Address changes may be possible depending on the shipment status and location. Additional charges may apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms. For business accounts, we also offer invoicing options. Please check our payment page for a comprehensive list of accepted methods.
