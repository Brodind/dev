FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries about our services and operations.

What types of packages do you deliver?

We deliver a wide range of packages, from small envelopes to large cargo shipments. Our services are designed to accommodate various delivery needs.

How can I track my package?

You can track your package using the tracking ID provided in your shipment confirmation email. Simply enter your ID on our website's tracking page to get real-time updates.

What are your delivery hours?

Our standard delivery hours are from 9am to 6pm, Monday through Friday. We also offer weekend and after-hours delivery options for urgent shipments.

What if I need to change my delivery address?

Address changes may be possible depending on the shipment status. Please contact our customer support as soon as possible with your tracking ID and new address details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide international shipping services to most countries worldwide. Please check our international shipping guide for more details on destinations and delivery times.
